OTHER TEMPLATES FOR LI_CRM
==========================

INVOICE_DEFAULT_CH1 : DEFAULT TEMPLATE WITH CH-(SWISS)-LAYOUT ONLY FOR A START > CHANGE IT FOR YOUR REQUIREMENT


USE IT WITHOUT WARRANTY
=======================

1. STEP

COPY THE TEMPLATE AND HIS CSS-FILE (E.G. invoice_default_ch1.html5 & invoice_default_ch1.css)
INTO THE ROOT_DIR/templates/ DIRECTORY

2. STEP

CREATE IN "CRM>Settings>Invoice template" A NEW INVOICE TEMPLATE WITH THOSE TEMPLATE.

3. STEP

SELECT THIS NEW NAMED TEMPLATE IN YOUR INVOICES
